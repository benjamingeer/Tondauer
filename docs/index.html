Tondauer

This web site is for experiments with digital editions of music.

Felix Mendelssohn, Prelude Op. 104a, No. 2 (MWV U 123)

These draft editions are intended to illustrate possible functionality. Source code is at https://github.com/dasch-swiss/Tondauer/.

  • Editions encoded and rendered with LilyPond, in PDF format, for printing or for use with a tablet:
    • Critical edition
    • Performance edition
    • Performance edition (variant)
  • Interactive critical edition (a very simple mockup without much functionality) encoded as MEI and rendered as SVG with Verovio

Digital facsimiles of sources:

  • ES1, the first German edition of V2, published in Leipzig by Bartholf Senff in 1868:
    • Reproduction from the Staatsbibliothek zu Berlin. Copyright © 2020 Staatsbibliothek zu Berlin. This reproduction is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International License.
    • Reproduction from the University of California Riverside Library. This reproduction is in the public domain.
  • EN, published in London by Novello in 1868 (not yet accessible here, for copyright reasons).
  • ES2 (reproduction from the Staatsbibliothek zu Berlin). A later Senff edition, published in about 1875, identical to ES1. Copyright © 2020 Staatsbibliothek zu Berlin. This reproduction is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International License.
  • EB (reproduction from IMSLP), the Breitkopf & Härtel edition of of V2, part of a critically revised edition of Mendelssohn's collected works, published between 1874 and 1877. This reproduction is in the public domain.
